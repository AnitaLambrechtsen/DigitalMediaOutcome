PAST PRODUCTIONS

CINDERELLA - 2021

A classic story told through our own lens at the Wellington Opera House. Through the leads of Cinderella, The Prince, her Evil Step Mother and Step Sisters, The Fairy Godmother and her three accompanying Fairies, this tale was able to delightfully come to life.

PETER PAN - 2020

With a ballet focus on this childhood story, our audience at both the Wellington Opera House and the Wellington Botanical Gardens, were able to witness Peter Pan, Tinkerbell, Wendy and her brothers, fly across the stage in this magical performance. Just make sure to stand well away from the menacing crocodile!


THE NUTCRACKER - 2019

For a ballet and Christmas favourite, our performance of The Nutcracker took place at both the Hannah Playhouse and Welly Christmas. Following the adventures of Clara, the Sugar Plum Fairy and the Nutcracker were able to guide her through the fantasy land filled with Russian Fudge to Turkish Delights.

OTHER PERFORMANCES


WELLY CHRISTMAS

An annual Christmas event in Wellington City is one that Tarrant Dance Studios is actively involved in. As well as participating in the famed Christmas Parade, the dancers are also given the opportunity to dance on the temporary stage during the event.

MATARIKI

The Wellington Matariki ki Pōneke celebration is also an annual event that Tarrant Dance Studios is involved in. With a combination of parades, dancing and improvisation, the senior dancers are often able to participate in this celebration of a new year in the Māori lunar calendar.
