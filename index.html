ABOUT US

Tarrant Dance Studios is located on Cuba Street, Wellington, under the Principal, Deirdre Tarrant. It is a studio with the focus of ballet, but also hosts a range of styles such as contemporary, hip hop and jazz for more senior dancers throughout the year.

Tarrant Dance Studios offers professional training for classical and contemporary dancers. At the heart of the Wellington Region, Tarrant Dance Studios is a company that immerses itself within regional performances and aid. Contrary to other ballet studios in the area, the dancers have frequent opportunities to build performance and presentation skills, through the many events that they are able to engage with. Some notable occasions are Welly Christmas and Matariki, which dancers are often annually able to perform in.

Tarrant Dance Studios is a company with the purpose of inspiring and instilling each member with a passion for dancing. Principal, Deirdre Tarrant, believes in improving a dynamic world through the art of dance.
