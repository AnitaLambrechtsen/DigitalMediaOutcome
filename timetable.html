TIMETABLE

At Tarrant Dance Studios, we teach from Primary to Advanced level Ballet. We follow the Royal Academy of Dance Examinations and schedule, and most students will choose to sit their RAD exams in the second-half of the year (although these examinations are not compulsory). The stages of ballet we teach follow in the descending order, Primary, Grades 1-5, Intermediate Foundation, Intermediate, Advanced Foundation, Advanced 1 and Advanced 2. The minimum age to join our primary class is 5 years old. From here, the minimum age to complete grades 1-5 is 7, then 11 for Intermediate Foundation, 12 for Intermediate, 13 for Advanced Foundation, 14 for Advanced 1 and 15 for Advanced 2. For those that are new to ballet, they will be appropriately placed based on their age. Our Scholar's classes are for those at Intermediate level or above.
